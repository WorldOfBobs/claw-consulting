Process

How we work.

Step 01

Understand the workflow

We start with the real process, not the fake polished version. Where do jobs come in, where does follow-up stall, and where would a Bobify dashboard give the clearest lift?

Step 02

Find the highest-friction bottleneck

We look for repetitive, expensive, or annoying steps that should move into one visible operating system instead of staying scattered across inboxes, calls, and spreadsheets.

Step 03

Build the smallest useful version fast

Usually that means a first Bobify build, one focused automation, or one reporting layer that solves a real daily pain fast for the office team.

Step 04

Refine from real use

Once people use it, the real needs show up fast. Then we harden the workflow, tighten the UI, and expand only where the value is obvious.
