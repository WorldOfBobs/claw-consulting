Process

How we work.

Step 01

Understand the workflow

We start with the real process, not the hypothetical future version. Where does work come in, where does it stall, and who has to touch it?

Step 02

Find the highest-friction bottleneck

We look for repetitive, expensive, or annoying steps that are obvious wins for automation or better tooling.

Step 03

Build the smallest useful version fast

Instead of a giant transformation project, we ship something useful quickly and make sure it earns the next layer of investment.

Step 04

Refine from real use

Once people actually touch it, the truth shows up fast. We use that feedback to harden the workflow and expand intelligently.
